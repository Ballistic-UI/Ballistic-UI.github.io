    • Recipe #1

  • Time: 45min
  • Serves: 10

    • Ingredients Quantity
    Brownie Mix: One Box
    Eggs: 2
    Milk/Water 3 1/4 cups

    Step 1:Prep materials

    Gather all the materials that are needed to create the perfect brownies.preheat oven to 400 farenheit

  • mixing bowl
  • Whisk/Mixer
  • All Ingredients
  • Pan
  • Spray Butter

    • Step 2: Make mix

    Pour the Brownie Mix into the mixing bowl along with the two eggs and 3 1/4 cup of milk or water

    Mix thouroughly until very thick but liquidy solution is established

    Step 3:Butter you Oven safe Pan

    By now the oven should have been preheated to 400 degrees. This will allow you to butter your oven safe pan and pour the brownie mix onto the pan.

    Step 4: Set cook

    Set the cook time for 15-20 minutes or until thoruouly cooked inside.

    Carefull it's hot

    Step 5: Remove the Pan

    Remove the pan from the oven with oven mits and set pan asside. let brownies cool

    Step 6: Enjoy

    cut the brownies into desired size and enjoy your deliciousness =)
